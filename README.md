## WELCOME TO CARD WARS 

-`How do I play?
```
You (The player) will compete agaisnt the computer and all you have to do is press 1 button!

`- There are 52 cards in a deck of cards and your onjective is to get them all.
`- Each player will start with 26 cards that are turned so you cannot see them
`- At the click of a button you and the computer will reveal your cards and whoever has the highest card wins both cards!
`- the winner is whoever gets all 52 cards first!
